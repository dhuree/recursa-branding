How Recursa Works

Four files. Infinite improvement.

LOOP.md

Define your iteration cycle. What's one unit of work? What happens each time?

METRICS.md

Track what matters. Quantify success so improvement becomes measurable.

LEARNING.md

Capture accumulated knowledge. What you learn persists across iterations.

SOUL.md

Establish identity and values. Consistent behavior across decisions.
